      The student-faculty ratio at Alfred University is 11:1, and the school has 67.1% of its classes with fewer than 20 students. The most popular majors at Alfred University include: Fine/Studio Arts, General; Mechanical Engineering; Business Administration and Management, General; Ceramic Sciences and Engineering; Psychology, General; Biology/Biological Sciences, General; Elementary Education and Teaching; Engineering, Other; Criminal Justice/Law Enforcement Administration; and Materials Engineering. The average freshman retention rate, an indicator of student satisfaction, is 70%.
